I have had the snow leopard Snowy for years; it's about 2 months older than I am, so 31 years old, and my bear Cuddles, which is 2 years younger, so it's 29 years old forever. They are my go-to when I am stressed, anxious, or even depressed. They have been a comfort for me for a long time and something I treasure and look for when I need calming or reassurance.

My headphones are important to me when I go out for walks or out into public and have to deal with buses and appointments and other people. Sometimes I get a bit anxious and my anxiety runs high so having my headphones on me helps especially to distract myself by either listening to music or watching videos on Facebook or TikTok.

My inhalers are helpful for when I need help to breathe. My daily is to be used in the mornings and at night. For my rescue inhaler. I use it when I need and usually that has been if my allergies act up, overworked or when I get high anxiety I need the extra help for my lungs.

Straws I noticed is something I start chewing on when I get stressed and anxious. It seems to help when it comes to distracting me and helps refocus or calm me down some. I have been doing it since I was young and just never seemed to have broken the habit.

I use wax melts and incense that help with a calming effect and create a calm atmosphere. Most of the time I love the scents but there are some like dragon's blood, lavendar, suede, and a couple of others that really help to calm me and make me rather relaxed.

Nos is my one energy drink I have everyday, mostly because I get caffeine migraines if I dont have some form of caffeine. It also has the least amount of sugar then some juices and other energy drinks. I am diabetic so it is something I have to watch out for when comes to sugar.

Popcorn is my number one go to snack food when I am anxious or stressed out. It has always been a comfort food and its one thing I dont stress on when comes to sugars or anything. Also makes my anxiety decrease.

Having my phone is important as when I get anxious I like to scroll videos on TikTok and Facebook that I will get lost in for hours at a time without realizing it. It is quite the distraction and helps me forget that I was even anxious in the first place.

My tablet I use the same as the phone but I also like to draw out my custom character illustrations as well as read some ebooks I have so that I can get lost in another world and relax for awhile.

My books I have been a fan of since I was little. For me books is a way to escape reality. Go into another world and live another life. It's an escape that many people use when stressed or just overwhelmed in general.

My blankets are like another form of security when I get highly anxious I will tend to curl up in them and wrap them around me for comfort or support. I also prefer to have soft blankets as it is soothing with the texture of the softness.

Having a fan always help as I get more aggreviated if I am really hot so when anxious having a fan on and keeping cool will help. Especially because sometimes when my anxiety goes up I get hot rather quickly which then doesnt help much just adds to my anxiety.

Having a hairtie is great as I seem to just wrap them around my wrists or my fingers and kinda fidget with them when I am anxious. It is something that helps even though I am not sure how it started in the first place.

My meds are a must just because I am diabetic so I need to make sure to take them everday and same with my allegy pills because I am allergic to all weeds, grass, trees, dust and pet dandruff as well as woods as much as that is all things I love.

My vape is important as I have noticed when I get highly stressed or my anxiety increases I tend to smoke more. I quit smoking actual cigarettes a bit over 2 years ago and had switched to vape, but I have noticed I still need the nicotine to help calm me down some.

My hoodie is something that is form fitting mostly minus the arms as they are always bigger then my arms and hands. It is something I like to curl up in and wrap myself in for that extra comfort and support.

Deodarant is important due to all the sweating when I get anxious and that fact that I can stand to sweat nor the smell.

Lotion is another thing I like to use from time to time. I have a Lavender and a Vanilla lotion that are both meant for soothing and relaxing. Their scent is quite strong and it actually does help with the calming aspect.

Having a light to read is great as well as just having just a tiny lamp to keep it not so dark but a calm relaxing lighting which can help when one becomes anxious because in the dark sometimes it can make it worse.

Having a couple pillows like small soft ones or even small squishy ones is great to cuddle up with and helps give extra comfort.
